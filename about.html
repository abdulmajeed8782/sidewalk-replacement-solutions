About Eden Contracting NY

Your Trusted Sidewalk Repair & DOT Violation Specialists

With years of experience serving New York City, we are your reliable partners for professional sidewalk repair, DOT violation removal, and concrete solutions throughout all five boroughs.

Our Story

Eden Contracting NY was founded with a simple mission: to provide New York City property owners with reliable, professional sidewalk repair and concrete services that ensure safety, compliance, and peace of mind. Based in the Bronx, we understand the unique challenges that NYC sidewalks face daily.

From the bustling streets of Manhattan to the residential neighborhoods of Queens, from the historic brownstones of Brooklyn to the diverse communities of the Bronx, we have built our reputation on quality workmanship, honest pricing, and exceptional customer service.

Our team of licensed and insured professionals brings years of experience in navigating NYC's complex DOT regulations, ensuring that every project we undertake meets the highest standards of safety and compliance.
