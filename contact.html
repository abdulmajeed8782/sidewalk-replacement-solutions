Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can you provide an estimate?

We typically provide estimates within 24 hours of your request. For urgent situations, we can often provide same-day estimates.

Do you handle DOT violations?

Yes, we specialize in DOT violation removal and work directly with the NYC Department of Transportation to ensure compliance.

Are you available for emergencies?

Absolutely. We provide 24/7 emergency sidewalk repair services for safety hazards that require immediate attention.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all five boroughs of New York City: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, and Staten Island.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed NYC contractors with comprehensive liability and workers' compensation insurance.

How long does sidewalk repair take?

Most sidewalk repairs can be completed within 1-3 days, depending on the scope of work and weather conditions.
